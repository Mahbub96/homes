    • Speech of Gain Passive Agreement : A persuasive speech in which the speaker's gola is to convince the audience that a given policy is desirable without encouraging the audience to take action in support of the policy.
    • Speech to Gain Immediate actin : A persuasive speech in which the speakers goal to convince the audience to take action in support of a given policy.
    • Persuasive speeches on question of policy are given whenever peaple debate specific course of action.
      To be effective , such speeches need to deal with the three issues of need , plan and practically.
    • Need : The First basic issues in analyzing a question of policy .Is there a serious problem or need that requires a change form current policy
    • Burden of Proof : The obelization facing a persuasive speaker prove that a change form current policy is necessary.
    • Plan : The second basic issue in analyzing a question of policy. if there is a problem in current policy , does the speaker have and plan to solve the problem?
    • Practically : The third basic issue is analyzing a question of policy,will the speakers plan solve the problem ? will it create new and more serious problem?
